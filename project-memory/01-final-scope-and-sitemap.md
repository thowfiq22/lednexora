## Important page decisions

- Do not include the old SAP MBC Guide as a fixed design page. Client said it is not a page to design.
- Do not recreate all current Zoho pages one-to-one. Zoho is included, but reduced to 3 important pages.
- Employee Onboarding Form is included as a dedicated dynamic functional page.
- Insights & Media Updates is the admin-managed content area for articles, media, short video links, and resource updates.
- Cookie consent may be implemented as a banner/component if required; it does not need to become a separate page unless requested.
